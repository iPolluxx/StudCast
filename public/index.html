Questions Contractors Ask

How accurate are the prices?
Every line is priced against your own supplier catalog first. Upload your CSV and the AI uses your real rates; only items you haven't priced fall back to a regional estimate, which you can edit before sending. You set the final number on every line.
Is my pricing data private?
Yes. Your price book is scoped to your account alone. Your supplier rates are never shared, pooled, or shown to other contractors.
What does my client actually receive?
A branded PDF with your logo, license number, and address; itemized material and labor tables; Wisconsin sales tax calculated automatically; a 30-day validity clause; and dual signature lines. It emails straight to your inbox to forward on.
Do I need to be good with computers?
No. Speak or type the job the way you'd describe it to a buddy. There are no forms to fill out. Review the ledger, adjust anything that needs it, and send.
What does it cost, and can I cancel?
$49 a month, flat. No contract, no setup fee, no per-estimate charge. Cancel anytime from your workspace.
